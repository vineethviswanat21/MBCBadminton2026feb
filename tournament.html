Format, schedule, rules, and payment information

Tournament Format

A total of 10 teams will participate and will be divided into two groups of five: Group A and Group B.

  • Each group will follow a round-robin format (teams play others within the same group).
  • Based on group standings, the top 4 teams from each group will qualify for the quarterfinals.

Quarterfinal Matchups (Only 1 Game)

  • Quarterfinal 1: A1 vs B4
  • Quarterfinal 2: B2 vs A3
  • Quarterfinal 3: B1 vs A4
  • Quarterfinal 4: A2 vs B3

Knockout Stage (Only 1 Game)

  • Winners of Quarterfinals 1 and 2 will advance to Semifinal 1.
  • Winners of Quarterfinals 3 and 4 will advance to Semifinal 2.

Finals (Best of 3)

Winners of the two semifinals will compete in the Final, played as a best-of-3 series.

This format ensures the winning team plays a maximum of 9 games, allowing the entire tournament to be completed in a single day.

Notes

  • Once enrolled, if a team fails to participate or skips the tournament, they will be counted among the last two teams.
  • If a team sustains an injury during the tournament, they will be allowed one rest break of up to 5 minutes. If unable to continue beyond this period, a decision may be taken to eliminate the team.
  • No substitutions will be permitted once the tournament has started.
  • The umpire’s decision will be considered final and binding.
  • The tournament will generally follow standard badminton rules and etiquette; however, they may not be enforced with absolute precision. Please keep in mind this is a friendly tournament and play in the right spirit.
