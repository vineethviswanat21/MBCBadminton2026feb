What an exciting day it was at the MBC Badminton Tournament held on Feb 22nd!
The tournament featured 10 dynamic teams, divided into 2 groups of 5 teams each. In the initial stage, teams battled it out within their groups in a round-robin format, with the top 4 teams from each group advancing to the quarterfinals.

After some thrilling knockout clashes, the competition intensified as Ram–Chary, Sanath–Guru, Vikas–Karthik, and Sasi–Vijay secured their spots in the semifinals. The semis were fiercely contested, showcasing exceptional teamwork and resilience.

The grand finale saw Vikas–Karthik face off against Sasi–Vijay in a nail-biting Best of 3 showdown. With both teams winning one match each, the decider went down to the wire — and in an electrifying finish, Vikas–Karthik clinched the championship with a 22–24 victory in the final match!

The energy, sportsmanship, and camaraderie on display made the event truly memorable. The day concluded with medal distributions, celebrating every participant’s effort and spirit.

After nearly 2 months of planning and discussions, it was wonderful to see the tournament come to life so successfully.

Here’s to many more such tournaments — already looking forward to the next one!
- Hail Martin!
